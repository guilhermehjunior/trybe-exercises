Fatos sobre o Stomatopoda

The mantis shrimp(Stomatopoda) lives in warm, shallow water and typically grows to be between 6-12 inches (15-30cm) in lenght

It has sixteen color receptive cones(humans have three).

It's one of the most creatively violent animals on earth.
