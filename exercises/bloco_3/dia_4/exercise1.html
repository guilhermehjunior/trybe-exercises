Fatos sobre o Stomatopoda

Informações Gerais

Nome Cientifico: Odontodactylus scyllarus

Reino:Animalia
Filo:Arthropoda
Subfilo:Crustacea
Classe:Malacostraca
Subclasse:Hopoclarida
Odem:Stomatopoda

Where it lives?

The mantis shrimp(Stomatopoda) lives in warm, shallow water and typically grows to be between 6-12 inches (15-30cm) in lenght

How many colors can it see?

It has sixteen color receptive cones(humans have three).

The violence

It's one of the most creatively violent animals on earth.
